Band Central ("we", "our", or "us") operates the Band Central website and mobile application. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our service.

1. Information We Collect

Account Information

When you create an account, we collect your email address and display name through Firebase Authentication. You may also sign in using third-party providers (such as Google), in which case we receive your name, email address, and profile photo URL from that provider.

User Content

We store content you create within the app, including songs, lyrics, setlists, tags, and related metadata. This content is stored in Google Cloud Firestore and is associated with your account.

Usage and Analytics Data

We use Google Analytics and Firebase Crashlytics to collect anonymized usage data and crash reports. This may include device type, operating system, app interactions, and performance data. This information helps us improve the app experience and fix issues.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain the Band Central service
  • To authenticate your identity and manage your account
  • To store and display your songs, lyrics, and setlists
  • To enable collaboration with other band members you invite
  • To analyze app usage and improve performance
  • To diagnose and fix technical issues

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. Your content is shared only with other members of bands you belong to, based on the roles and permissions you configure. We use the following third-party services to operate Band Central:

  • Google Firebase - Authentication, database, hosting, and cloud functions
  • Google Analytics - Anonymized usage analytics
  • Firebase Crashlytics - Crash reporting and diagnostics

4. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored on Google Cloud infrastructure through Firebase. We use industry-standard security measures including encrypted connections (HTTPS), Firebase Security Rules to control data access, and role-based permissions within the application.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Update or correct your account information
  • Delete your account and associated data by contacting us
  • Opt out of analytics tracking through your device settings

6. Children's Privacy

Band Central is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that we have collected personal data from a child under 13, we will take steps to delete that information.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
